Cart on an Inclined Plane

The two lines perpendicular to the track in the applet mark the points at which the cart is at its equilibrium position when flat and when the cart is at its equilibrium position when at an angle. To do this applet successfully (e.g. get the completion code), you must properly orient the four force vectors shown (friction, gravity, normal, spring). To know how to orient the vectors you need to know the initial conditions, e.g. the cart is to the left of the equilibrium point and moving to the left.

To orient one of the force vectors, hold down "Shift" and left-click with the mouse on that vector, and move the tip of the force vector to the position appropriate to this situation by dragging the mouse. Hit the "test" button in the applet to see if you are correct. The applet will then tell you which forces are correctly oriented and which ones are not. If you do not get them all correct, reposition the incorrect vector(s) and try again. When they are all in the correct position for your initial position, you will be given the completion code.
