Faradays Law: Rotating Ring

We have a ring which sits in a horizontal background magnetic field. To rotate the ring, change the horizontal field component (postive is to the right), or change the radius of the ring, use the top three sliders.


The ring has a self-inductance and a resistance. We can change the resistance of the ring using the bottom slider.


The top graph to the right shows the external magnetic flux and the total magnetic flux. The graph below shows the induced (eddy) current in the ring.
