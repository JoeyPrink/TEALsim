Execution Control

The simulation is controled by the execution control buttons at the bottom of the view window. These buttons have the properties indicated below.


View Control

The view controls are as follows. To rotate, left-click and drag in the view window. To translate the view perpendicular to the camera view, right-click and drag in the view window. To zoom in and out, alt-left-click and drag in the view window or middle-button-click and drag. Some of these properties may be disabled, depending on the application.
