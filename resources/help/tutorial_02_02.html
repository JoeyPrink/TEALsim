Tutorial_02_02:

A simulation of a coil falling under gravity and bouncing off of a floor. We also create a graph of the vertical position of the coil as a function of time. In addition, we add a slider that allows us to vary the amount of damping in the world. By setting the slider to a non-zero value, the user can get the height of the coil to decay exponentially. The user can set the amount of damping either by moving the slider or by entering a value in the box to the right of the slider AND HITTING ENTER.
