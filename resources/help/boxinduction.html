Charging by Induction:

This application illustrates how to charge a conducting object by induction. The user can charge the sphere external to the square conductor by using the slider labeled "external charge". The square conductor can be grounded by using the button so labeled, and ungrounded by using the corresponding button.

The control buttons at the bottom of the view window have the properties indicated on the figure below.

The view controls are as follow. To translate the view perpendicular to the camera view, right-click and drag in the view window. To zoom in and out, alt-left-click and drag in the view window or middle-button-click and drag. To rotate (this may not be allowed), left-click and drag in the view window.
