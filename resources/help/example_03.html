Example_03:

A simulation of a charge falling under gravity and bouncing off of a floor. We also create a graph of the vertical position of the charge as a function of time. In addition, we add a slider that allows us to vary the amount of damping in the world.

By setting the slider to a non-zero value, the user can get the height of the charge to decay exponentially. The user can set the amount of damping either by moving the slider or by entering a value in the box to the right of the slider AND HITTING ENTER.
